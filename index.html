Welcome to Destiny of Music

  Established in 1998, Destiny of Music is a multi-sectoral provider of education with faculties of Vocational Education and Training, Higher Education and Secondary Education. Destiny of Music is committed to providing the highest quality education to enable and empower its students to seek self-improvement, irrespective of nationality, gender or belief.

  The Institute is a quality provider of secondary, vocational and higher education, with a focus on both domestic and international students. By the dedicated pursuit of best practice teaching and the provision of a dynamic, student-centred learning environment Destiny of Music fosters in its students rational thought, intellectual integrity and social responsibility.
